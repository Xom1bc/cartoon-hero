Charmander

About Charmander

Charmander is an awesome Pokemon! They are my favourite starter and the coolest by far. They are a fire type Pokemon and is one of the three starters from the kanto region. They are buddies with the other starters, squirtle and bulbasaur.

Charmander and his friends

Charmander's Characteristics

  1. They have long tails.
  2. Their tails have fire at the end
  3. The fire is their source of life, so Charmanders don't often like the rain.
